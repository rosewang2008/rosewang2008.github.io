Bio

I am a researcher at OpenAI :)

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University advised by Diyi Yang and Dora Demszky where my work is recognized by the 2025 Economic Report of the President. Previously, I earned my B.Sc. in Computer Science from MIT where I worked with Profs. Josh Tenenbaum and Jonathan How, and interned at Google Brain Robotics (2019), Google Brain (2020), and Ai2 (2024).
